Services

Exterior Painting

At Wollet Painting we have over twenty-five years of experience delivering the highest quality work on both residential homes and commercial properties, both existing and newly constructed, ranging from front doors to condiminium complexes. The general stages of work that occur on exterior projects include the following outlined in your proposal.

  • Power Washing
  • Surface Preperation
  • Prime and Paint/Stain
  • Cleanup
  • Inspection

Interior Painting

Over twenty-five years of experience working on both residential homes and commercial properties, both existing and newly constructed, with interior projects ranging from foyers to executive offices. The general stages of work that occur on interior projects include the following along with any special needs or directions as specified in your proposal.

  • Setup
  • Preparation
  • Prime and Paint
  • Cleanup
  • Inspection

Power Washing

Over twenty-five years of experience power washing both residential homes and commercial properties ranging from garages to shopping malls and plazas, both maintaining the appearance and extending the life of property. The stages of work that occur on power washing projects include the following along with any special needs or instructions.

  • Preparation
  • Apply Cleaners
  • Hand Scrub and Flush Gutters
  • High-Pressure Wash
  • Inspection

About

Experienced Professional Contractors

If you are like most people and find the work toward finding a painter that you can trust with high-quality service with no hidden charges and guarantees their work, here are some things to look for in a painting contractor.

  • Provides you with actual receipts
  • Requires payment only after work is completed and inspected
  • Can show you current proof of insurance and provide references
  • Is a full-time established company with a good reputation
  • Has good business relationships with other service vendors
